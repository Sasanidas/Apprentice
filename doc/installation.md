## Installation

### Requirements

  * [Emacs](https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/) 24.4 or later
  * [Elixir](http://elixir-lang.org/) 1.0 or later

### Manual

You can install Apprentice manually by placing Apprentice on your `load-path` and
`require` ing it. Many people favour the folder `~/.emacs.d/vendor`.

```el
(add-to-list 'load-path "~/.emacs.d/vendor/apprentice.el/")
(require 'apprentice)
```
